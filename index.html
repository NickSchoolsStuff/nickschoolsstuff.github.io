Workshops

Every month there will be one to two workshops following a set of themes. In these workshops you will be able to express your creativity with your friends. you will be provided materials to make your own board games, candies, and t-shirts; participate in contests and events for prizes; and be provided the opportunity to either keep your creations or donate them to a good cause.

The Woodstock Workshop

Workshop to build bird feeders, miniature garden beds, and anything wood. people will be able to cut with tools and supplied the help to build their own eco-friendly things.Returned bird feeders will be used around school and at local parks while gardening things may come in useful in the fishbowl

High-Tech Workshop

Build a website and try 3d modeling. The websites will be judged by your peers and first place wins a prize. Your 3d trinkets will be delivered to you within the next week.

Paperfest Workshop

Make origami creations with your friends and try making paper mache sculptures. Build your own board game with your own figurines, Play with your own against your friends for a prize that we will provide.

Winterfun Workshop

Make various candies and build a gingerbread house. Partake in a white elephant event (free to all), and try making a small quilt to stay warm for the holidays.

Tye-dye

Try Making your own t-shirt design from scratch! learn to tie-dye your own design, press small patterns, and make the design your own with a cross-stitch. At the end you will have the option to donate it if you like so someone else can enjoy your creation.

Trash into treasure

Use the world around you as your canvas. Make cool mosaics from recycled materials ,paint cute pet rocks, and give old things new life. The most creative designs get a prize (100% Recycled of course).

Minatures Workshop

Learn to make your very own miniature models. At your scale no object is too small to replicate, be it figurines, rivers, or even the school. Breath life into a smaller scale, just try it, its a small step in the right direction.

In these virtual parties you will play against your friends in games you love for a prize pool of various gifts or money. Gaming Tournaments

My goals for these tournaments will be to make them not cringy and fun. Therefore I believe that a good portions of the funds should ensure that the atmosphere is professional and fun. This means dedicated servers and gamemodes for games that natively support a competative PvP experience. For other games this will be done via a bingo system where points are accumulated through completing tasks and the person/team with the most points by the end of the compition wins. The winner will be also given the option to multiply his/her winnings to donate to a charity of their choice.
